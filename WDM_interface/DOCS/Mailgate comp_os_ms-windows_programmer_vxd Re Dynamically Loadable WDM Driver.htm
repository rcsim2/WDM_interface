"Rik G." wrote:
> Could anyone please tell me what's the equivalent of dynamically loadable
> VxD's (CreateFile/DeleteFile) in the WDM?

This is one thing you can't do with WDM drivers in Win98. In Win2K, you
can use the service control manager to load and unload drivers, but
Win98 doesn't have such a beast. The reason for this restriction is
basically that WDM drivers are intended just for hardware. To get
kernel-mode support for an application, you should continue to use a
VxD.
