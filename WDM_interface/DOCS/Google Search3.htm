You're right that the "simple NT driver" he described isn't a WDM driver, 
since it doesn't included PNP support. 

But what I heard the original poster ask was "is it possible to write a WDM 
driver that does what my simple NT driver does, so that this WDM driver 
works under both NT 5 and W98?" 

The answer to that is yes. And in fact it won't take much work for him to 
add the PNP support to his simple NT driver to transform it into a full WDM
driver.
